Monthly Screenings

Documentary Airways hosts monthly screenings of quality documentary films for the selected group of foreign audiences from diverse cultural background living in Seoul. For the foreign documentary fans, it’ll be a unique opportunity to watch the latest Korean and Asian documentaries with English subtitles, and the filmmakers will be able to receive feedbacks from the global audiences.
