Docs Port Incheon Report

19 November 2014

Documentary Airways and its films were successfully launched at the second edition of Docs Port Incheon (Asian Documentary Project Market) was held from November 1st to 5th, 2014.

THE HOSPICE was screened as the opening showcase and was received with much applause by the audience composed of documentary professionals at fields.

Our two films, WIND ON THE MOON and FACTORY COMPLEX was pitched at the Rough Cut Sales section, and the award for Best Rough Cut Project was given to FACTORY COMPLEX, with the cash prize of KRW10,000,000 and the translation and subtitling support from Furmo DT.

Meanwhile, EVAPORATED was introduced in the K-Pitch section, and was awarded the Best Young Korean Director’s Project with the cash prize of KRW10,000,000 and post-production support from Digital Studio 2L. Furthermore, Jin Jin Pictures and Arthouse Momo promised theatrical release supports upon the completion of the film.

For more information on winners from 2014 Docs Port Incheon, please refer to the following link: http://idocs-port.org/idocs/document/133
